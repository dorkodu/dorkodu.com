The Dorkodu Manifesto

A Beginners Guide to Dorkodu

This manifesto is the summary of how we put our values into practice. Our work at Dorkodu is measured against the highest possible standards of ethical business conduct. Our commitment to our deepest values helps us hire great people, build great products, and attract loyal users.

Author

Doruk Eray — Founder and Chief

Our Culture

Dorkodu is not a conventional company. We do not intend to be one. Throughout Dorkodu's evolution, we have always tried to be unique. We have also emphasized an atmosphere of creativity, freedom and challenge, which helps us providing people unbiased, accurate and free access to useful and meaningful knowledge.

I founded Dorkodu because I believed we could provide a service to the humanity - instantly delivering true and useful information on any topic. Serving our users is at the heart of what we do, and remains our number one priority.

We create products that we are proud of, and we hope in the future they will have even greater positive impact on the humanity.

Business, Risks and Rewards

If opportunities arise that might cause us to sacrifice short term results but are in the best long term interest of our users, we will take those opportunities.

We make business decisions with the long term welfare of our company, users and shareholders in mind, but not based on accounting considerations.

Our business environment changes rapidly and needs long-term investment. We won't hesitate to place major bets on promising new opportunities.

We won't shy away from risky but high-reward projects because of the short-term earnings pressure. This means we need a growing income to satisfy our growing ambitions in our revolutionary work.

Executive Roles & Structure

We have a flat-like hierarchy in the company. We don't have industry-standart, cliche titles and management patterns. Our way is unique; simple but effective. As always how we desire.

For each high-level need that we have in the company, we create a branch. We list the specific missions, goals and requirements for that branch. Then we assign them to squads. One's roles can change but the purpose and mission in the foundation can't.

Each squad has a leader, who is the most experienced person that can see the big picture without missing the details. Everyone reports to their squad leader.

If you are a squad leader, you report to your branch lead. Branch leads report to the "board of leads" which they are also the members of.

The Chief is the true leader of the foundation and makes decisions under the guidance of the board of leads.

Work Life

Dorkodu is not so much a job as it is a way of life. You don't forget about work at the end of a day, when you leave work you still think about it; not because you have deadlines or because you have to, but because you are excited about what you are doing.

Everyone has a mission based on their experience, personality and knowledge. Tasks and roles are assigned you based on your mission and purpose in your squad.

Your role is limited only by your energy and passion, not by your title.

If you want to create a new product, it is yours to build. If you want to contribute on another product, just speak up. The energy and passion of the people working around you here is a constant reminder of how much we all believe in what we are doing.

For example, even if you are a graphic designer, you can work with engineers on a technical project. It doesn't matter if you don't have expertise in that area, if you feel interest and passion about that, go that way. Your creative and useful ideas will be appreciated.

Dorkodians

Our workmates are everything. Everyone at Dorkodu is called a "Dorkodian". We are focused on attracting and leveraging the talent of exceptional thinkers. We have been lucky to see many creative, principled, idealist, determined, high-working people.

Because of our talents, we can do exciting work in every knowledge-related field. Talented people are interested in Dorkodu because we empower them to change the destiny of human civilization.

Our main benefit is a workplace with meaningful projects, where our workmates can contribute and grow while enjoying the feeling of creating the tomorrow. Those contributions make Dorkodu better in our great journey.

20% ~ Create Your Masterpiece

 We encourage our workmates, in addition to their 80% regular work time, to spend 20% of their time working on what they think will most benefit the humanity. This lets them to be more innovative and creative. Many of our advances have happened in this manner. 

Our Mission

When I founded Dorkodu, I hoped it would reach a universal influence. Our intense and interest was to help people find, discover, exchange and use pure and true knowledge.

We believe that for a well-functioning humanity there should be an open and free resource for true, useful and meaningful information. This will be a game-changer in the future, for the destiny of human civilisation.

In pursuing our ambitious goals, we will always be mindful of our responsibilities to our users, workmates and shareholders. With our products, Dorkodu connects the human with knowledge.

This is a critical mission that should be accomplished by a dedicated, trustworthy and independent foundation.

Conclusion

Therefore, as Dorkodu we know we have a significant responsibility to the humanity. We will do our best making Dorkodu a long term success to give humanity a better tomorrow with the wisdom of knowledge.

the Dorkodu Company
