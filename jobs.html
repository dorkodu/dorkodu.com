Work @ Dorkodu

We want Dorkodu to be the foundation that will change the destiny of human civilization. So we need extraordinary, passionate and hard-working people to pursue this goal.

Opportunity is Knocking!

We are on a mission to liberate the knowledge & make it open, useful and meaningful for everyone. We will change the way information is being perceived, created, consumed and shared; thus improving the lives of entire human race.

Dorkodu is a startup based in Istanbul. We are working on a few products to pursue our mission. We started off as a small squad, but now we're growing rapidly. We're focused on just being humble, lean, smart and quick with our hacker mentality. So anyone who joins now can see the future before even others can dream about it.

At Dorkodu, our strategy is simple. We hire the most knowledgable and passionate people we can find.

We place great emphasis on maintaining a humble and intellectually honest environment where everyone's opinion is respected and where people's contributions are acknowledged and rewarded.

Our Work Culture

Opportunity is Knocking Join us! As a team, we are NOT optimising for happiness. Our goal is to maximise meaning. We don’t have a fancy office, don’t offer free meals, back-rubs or any other perks you can find in the industry. We wish, but not yet. What we offer is the adventure of your lifetime. - The chance to work on a product that can significantly improve society. - The chance to do the best work of your life.

Be bright. Be you.

Great Product. Happy Customers. It all starts with the right team, which in turn starts with awesome people. We are looking for purposeful, ambitious, self-driven individuals, who are passionate about the problem we are working on. We don’t have a schedule. You can contribute when you feel you are most productive. And most of the team is remote. That has some advantages but it comes with responsibilities: Take ownership We step up and get stuff done. We all know that things will not happen unless we work for it. There are tons of projects that need to get done and we all apply our skills to advance them. Show your work Results matter more than anything. We let the work do the talking most of the time. Act with urgency We’re focused on making quick decisions and executing fast. “How can this be done sooner?” is the mantra which we live by. Over-communicate We speak up & have no patience for silent disagreements. We prefer healthy debates. And we all let our colleagues know what goes through our minds. Why the hell would you sign up for something like this? Seems like a demanding job description, doesn’t it? Why would you do it? - Because you need a purpose in your life. Because working a 9-5 job, in large team, detached by the effects of your efforts sounds awful to you. Because you want your work to matter! - Because you understand that assuming responsibility is the only way to grow and develop yourself. Muscles don’t grow while you Netflix and chill on your sofa, but through the pain and sweat in the gym. The same is true for your career and your life. - Because you will be part of an awesome team of diverse top-performing individuals. And you understand that an entourage of self-driven people is the best gift for your future. - Because you are intelligent to know to never surrender your opportunity for security. You can always get a cozy secure job, but opportunities to join greatness are far in between. On the benefits side: - Be an early employee. - Join our high-growth startup to learn new skills and fast track your career growth; grow to be a company leader in the future, get management opportunities and run your team. - Market-level salary. - You can make your own schedule. - (As long as things happen on time and on spec.) - Remote first: We don’t care when and where you work from. - Potential for stock options package.

Our iterative work process helps us to think better, create better. We can achieve faster without trading off quality.

We debate, decide then commit. We move fast and stay lean, but act with purpose. We bring intent and insight to every work we do. We know we won't always get it right, but we are not afraid to learn from experiences.

    Product

  • Software Engineer

    We are seeking an experienced Software Engineer to join the Product squad. You will help build the next-generation system behind Dorkodu's products, create web applications that can reach millions of people, and build high volume services.

    Your Role
    • Code complex software using primarily PHP, JavaScript and preferably C/C++ or Go or *insert your favorite language here*.
    • Design, architect, develop and test software components.
    • Maintain architecture, spread design principles and philosophies in the team.
    • Partner with other squads to create internal tools for our growth.
    Our Expectations
    • Must have 4+ years of software engineering experience, or equivalent determination, curiosity and passion.
    • Mid-level knowledge of Computer Science. Understanding of the philosophy behind software engineering.
    • Knowledge of data storage (file system, memory, relational or NoSQL databases etc.)
    • Expertise with a few programming paradigms and languages like C++, C#, Java, Go, Haskell, Rust etc.
    • Knowledge of scripting languages like PHP or Python or JavaScript.
    • Experience with Git
    Bonus
    • Experience with *nix environments, C/C++, data engineering, artificial intelligence, information retrieval, network programming or developing large software systems a plus
    • Activity in open source, at least with a hobby project.

  • Product Designer

    We are looking for an experienced, creative and passionate Product Designer to play a key role in our engineering and art work so you should be able to write a little code to prototype what you design.

    Your Role
    • Be at the intersection of art and engineering to design and prototype new features for our unique products.
    • Design and ship products of the highest quality with the clean, simple and awesome style for which Dorkodu is known.
    • Take broad, conceptual ideas and turn them into something useful and valuable for our unique products.
    • Define, craft and evolve designs within visual systems and/or standards.
    Our Expectations
    • Strong portfolio featuring your design samples or self-launched products.
    • Experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Just a little backend experience as a plus.
    • 3+ years of experience building web, mobile or desktop applications.
    • Design skills with proficiency in typography, UI, color, layout, iconography and aesthetic sense and how these elements impact user experience.

  • Web Developer

    Dorkodu is seeking an experienced Web Developer, who will help bring new product ideas to life by developing awesome web apps. You must be able to independently write the code necessary to implement features.

    Your Role
    • Create awesome web apps using our frontend JavaScript environment : Lucid, Sage, Luckt.
    • Implement beautiful UI's using HTML + CSS + JavaScript.
    • Take experience designs and turn them into something alive, like a web app.
    Our Expectations
    • Are you fluent in acronyms? HTML, CSS, JS, PHP. Can you make them work together with your eyes closed?
    • Experience with building single page web apps (SPAs) using JavaScript. Especially with Lucid.js, but a library like Vue or React is also fine.
    • Must have at least 2 years experience, with either a solid work background or a great portfolio of self-started projects.

  • Graphic Designer

    We're a small startup and we have big graphic design needs. So we are looking for an exceptional Graphic Designer. The right candidate will be enthusiastic about supporting engineering, product design and marketing with superior design masterpieces.

    Your Role
    • Design high-impact, result-oriented, polished ad designs, presentations, materials, web pages for Product, Marketing and Operations.
    • Partner with Product squad on new designs and features for our awesome products.
    • Define, craft and evolve designs within our visual systems and/or standards.
    Our Expectations
    • Must have at least 2+ years experience, with either a work background or a killer design portfolio.
    • Ability to work in a chaotic, unstructured environment while delivering results.
    • Must not be afraid to do a little coding to make the job faster and easier.
    • Advanced skills in a variety of design tools, screen and print, like Photoshop/GIMP, Illustrator/Inkscape, Figma...
    • Ability to create great designs from concept to final deliverables with all of the excitement and pressure of a startup environment.

  • Experience Designer (UI/UX)

    Dorkodu is seeking an Experience Designer for our products. You will be a key member in a small, very talented group of software product developers. You will be responsible for defining and building the complete user experience and user interface. You will work very closely with the product designers and engineers to define and build detailed product specs.

    Your Role
    • Design and build the interface architecture.
    • Define use cases and information architecture.
    • Maintain and advance product style guidelines.
    • Build conceptual models, process flows, navigational maps, and wireframes.
    • Work across a wide set of technologies in small squads to deliver products over short development cycles.
    Our Expectations
    • Must have at least 2+ years experience, with either a work background or a killer design portfolio.
    • Expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and just a little backend development.
    • Proficiency in Graphic Design and Prototyping.
    • Experience designing and implementing user interfaces for scalable web products.
    • Familiarity with related web technologies (e.g. SPA, GraphQL, Sage, REST, PWA)

    Your Role

    • Clear verbal communicator and detail oriented.
    • Understanding of a little business, sales, finance, economy, product design
    • Experience with coding, user testing and complicated workflow design.

  • Postmaster

    The Postmaster role is a blend of operations and product management. However, the individual taking this role will have a technical operations experience. You will be responsible for tracking our website traffic, delivery of emails, managing our brand on social media. More, you will make PR experience better, which is essential for continued growth.

    Your Role
    • Administration of Dorkodu PR systems (emails, contacts, social media).
    • Track and analyse website traffic and growth, help Marketing squad running digital advertising campaigns.
    • Improve our brand value on social media.
    Our Expectations
    • Must be cautious, a clear thinker, detail-oriented, highly-focused.
    • The best candidate will likely have experience in programming, using Linux, systems administration and will be comfortable in automating operations using scripting such as PHP or JS or Python or Shell.
