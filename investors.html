Our Business

We bootstrapped Dorkodu by ourselves, as a side-hustle to our school life. We held the meetings at our high school during breaks, and mostly online during the pandemic. Thanks to our own minimalist engineering approach & architecture; we only needed to rent a shared hosting.

But we are aware of our potential to do really big things. Although we are not incorporated yet, sooner we will need to.
