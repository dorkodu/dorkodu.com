Our Principles and Values

Our values are what we stand for. They are our compass that guides us to the right direction in the sea of chaos. We believe in our values deeply and make sacrifices to pursue them.

  • Be Unique

    At Dorkodu, everyone feels valued. Uniqueness and diversity is not only embraced, it's proudly encouraged.

  • Knowledge and Truth

    Truth is the only ruler of reality. Our civilisation has an infinite knowledge heritage. We are committed to make it open, useful and meaningful for everyone.

  • Create the New

    The only truth is in the change. Will we wait for the tomorrow? No. We work to dream and create the future.

  • Give Meaning

    Meaning is an ultimate power of human-beings. We can use it to make sense of our lives, work for the better.

  • Be a Human, Learn and Feel

    Don't ignore your human-being. There is much to learn. Feel the life. See the truth in your thoughts and feelings.

  • Create Opportunity

    We know we are here because of others. So, if we have something, we use it to create an opportunity to touch everyone.
